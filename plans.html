Questions
Can I switch plans at any time?
Yes — upgrade or downgrade anytime. If you upgrade mid-cycle you'll only be charged the difference. If you downgrade, the change takes effect at the end of your billing period.
What happens to my binders if I downgrade to Free?
Your data is never deleted. If you downgrade to Free, your extra binders become read-only until you resubscribe. Your first binder stays fully active.
Is there a free trial for paid plans?
Yes — both Collector and Master come with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start. We'll remind you 3 days before the trial ends.
What does "early access to upcoming sets" mean?
Master members get new sets added to CoastiesBinder as soon as card data is available — typically weeks before the official release date. You can start planning your collection and marking wishlist cards before the set even drops.
